Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about .

  • Where can I find information on new features in Niri Cloud??

    You can learn all about the latest features in Niri Cloud on the overview page. To find out what’s in the latest version of each Niri Cloud desktop app, check out each product’s page on niriglobal.com.

  • When does my membership begin?

    If you purchase your individual membership from a retailer or an online store, it begins when you enter your redemption code. If you purchase a team membership through a reseller, your membership begins when you add seats to your account.

  • What happens to my benefits if I decide to stop my membership?

    You continue to have access to Niri Cloud member free benefits. And, if you saved your work to your computer, you continue to have access to those files on your own device. You no longer have access to the Niri Cloud apps or most of the services that are components of a Niri Cloud membership.

  • What’s included with a free Creative Cloud membership?

    A free niri Cloud membership gives you access to file syncing and sharing features and 2 GB of storage space. You also get some free services and access to free trials of the Niri Cloud desktop application

  • Can I exchange my order instead of returning it?

    As a Niri Global customer you have the option of exchanging an eligible item instead of returning it for a refund. You may be able to process an exchange yourself for certain eligible items in your Niri Global account.
